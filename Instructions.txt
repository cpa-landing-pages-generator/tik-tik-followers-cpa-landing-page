Installing your content locker URL on the php version landing pages is very easy.


1. Go to the folder "parts"
2. Edit verification.php
3. At the bottom, find "REPLACE YOUR CONTENT LOCKER URL HERE"
4. Replace it with your content locker URL.
5. Save it!


Example:

<a id="verification-button" class="tkt-btn animated pulse infinite" href="REPLACE YOUR CONTENT LOCKER URL HERE"><span>Verify Now</span></a>

Result:

<a id="verification-button" class="tkt-btn animated pulse infinite" href="https://verifyzone.net/aa12bb34cc56dd78"><span>Verify Now</span></a>
